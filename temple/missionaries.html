Full-Time Temple Missionary

The hotel count with long-term stay accommodations in kitchenette suites for full-time temple missionaries. We have a range of hotel options to ensure you find the best lodging for your extended stay. You can get the following services:

  • on-site laundry facilities
  • Free WiFi
  • Residential-style studio and one-bedroom suites
  • Business center
  • Weekly housekeeping
  • Free parking
  • One- and two-bedroom suites
  • Hotel support (24/7 staff) and amenities

Restaurant Service

If you do not have enough time to cook, you can purchase our restaurant service, where you can find typical food of the region and typical American food. We adjust your meals to your diet and nutritional requirements. The hotels offer the following food service:
