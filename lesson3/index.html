site purpose

Chia means moon in the native language. Moon's road is a chamber of commerce whose purpose is to help small and medium businesses to grow through commercial agreements. In my country and my city, there are no business communities; this is an opportunity to create networks and share ideas and goals. This website is going to show how the chamber of commerce works, and help businesses to join us. Our goal is to help our City, Chia, to improve its commerce and create benefits between businesses.
