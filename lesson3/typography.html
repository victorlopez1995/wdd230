site purpose

I am Victor López, I am a computer programmer. I am from Colombia. In my country, there are so many indigenous and Afro-descendant communities, They work making handicraft objects, but often their work is underestimated or it is not possible to find. Where can you learn about their culture and purchase their products? My website is going to show these communities’ work, and help people buy it easily. My goal is to help these communities to be seen and valued in this world.
