Target Audience

Our target customers are small and medium businesses from Chia, Colombia. We want to offer a space where our local businesses can offer their products and services by motivating the communication between them. So, our customers could have a simple financial situation in their businesses. Our target customers should enjoy sharing their experiences and ideas to help us to grow. They need more information about what they have to do to participate in our community (product, places, prices, and so on). It expected they could access our website through their laptops and mobile device.

Todopan Chia is a local business dedicated to selling products for bakeries and cake shops. This business offers capacitation to people here in Chia to create their own cake businesses. Todopan Chia is 20 years old, and it is one of the biggest businesses in Chia.
